About Me

My research lies in the application of machine learning in healthcare, specifically in information extraction from medical datasets with low inter-class variance. I've worked with various medical imaging modalities and am keen on exploring multimodal representation learning for patient phenotyping and monitoring disease progression.

Research Interests

  • Multimodal representation learning of patient data
  • Self-supervised learning for healthcare data
  • Information extraction from medical data with low inter-class variance
